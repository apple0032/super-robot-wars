    How to play?

  1. NEW GAME button will generate new map grid with Players stat boxes
  2. To navigate through map grid, use right mouse click
  3. Boxes on which Penguin can relocate are highlighted
  4. When Penguin enters box with a weapon, it is picked up and old weapon is left on its place
  5. When Penguins enter boxes directly next to each other, battle begins
  6. If active Penguin attacks - it induces damage on opponents health points according to weapon's damage value
  7. If active Penguin defends - damage induced by opponent in next turn will be divided by half
  8. When any Penguin's healthpoints drop to 0, battle ends and game is over
